How to Relax before your Journalism Finals (So You Can Ace Them!)

Time Required:One Hour (Or however long you can spare!)
Awesome Rating: 100% awesome

Are you stressing becuase of your Journalism final? Or because of any final?? I'm going to show you how to chillax before you take jump into the tests so you have a clear mind, ready to ACE them!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Warm Bath
  • Essential Oils
  • A Warm Drink, like Tea!
  • Chocolate
  • Calming Music

  1. Soak in warm bath.

    Warm water can help to release endorphins that reduce stress, so take a hot bubble bath for twenty minutes.

  2. Try Aromatherapy with Essential Oils.

    Many popular aromatherapy smells include lavender, peppermint, and eucalyptus.

  3. Drink warm liquids.

    Hot drinks help to relieve tension and reduce stress. Look for hot drinks that lack caffeine and alcohol, as these can stimulate anxiety and depression.

  4. Eat some chocolate.

    This may sound wierd but its true! Chocolate has calming properties and will but you into a relaxing mood just by eating it.

  5. Listen to calming music.

    The final step to relax is to just chillax and listen to some calming music. There are a lot of calming music choices on YouTube. Just pick your favorite, close your eyes, and let the waves of calm music notes roll over you. Here's an example!
